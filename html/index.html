At New Beginnings, we provide compassionate, evidence-based treatment to help
individuals overcome alcohol addiction and reclaim their lives.

Our treatment programmes

Flexible

Outpatient Programs

For those who need to maintain work or family commitments while receiving treatment, we offer flexible outpatient programs at various intensity levels.

Therapy

Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions with licensed therapists to address the root causes of addiction and develop personalized coping strategies.

Community

Group Therapy

Peer support groups facilitated by addiction specialists to share experiences, build connections, and learn from others in recovery.

Family

Family Programs

We help heal relationships damaged by addiction through family therapy sessions and educational programs for loved ones.

Aftercare

Continuing Care

Our commitment to your recovery continues after treatment with alumni programs, sober living referrals, and ongoing support groups.

30-90 day

Residential treatment

Our comprehensive inpatient program provides 24/7 care in a supportive, structured environment with medical supervision, therapy, and life skills training.
